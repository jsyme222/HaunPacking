Our
Onions

Our  onions ship in
25 lb bags, 50 lb bags,
or in 40 lb boxes

The growing process starts with preparing the fields. Under a watchful eye, the growers, prep their fields. After years of experience they monitor weather conditions, soil temperatures, and water availability to determine the optimum time to begin planting. Seed selection is a key ingredient in insuring that you have a premium onion.

Once the onions are harvested, Haun Packing closely monitors the curing and storing of the onions. With Idaho having a dry warm climate, Weiser, Idaho has proven to be the ideal place for curing and storing onions.

Jumbo Red,
